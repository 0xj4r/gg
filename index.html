Josh Ransom

About

Josh Ransom is the Director of Software Engineering at Sweetbridge where he leads development teams responsible for creating applications and technical protocols that make up the Sweetbridge ecosystem. Prior to working with Sweetbridge, Josh worked at American Express where he architected and heavily contributed a full stack platform for enabling small teams the capability to rapidly deliver globally scalable financial web and mobile applications. Josh gets his background in supply chain from Intel where he built software instrumental to the success of the company’s global supply chain responsible for nearly 60 billion dollars in annual revenue. He is heavily involved in the Phoenix communities for blockchain and software development in addition to being a proud Computer Science alumni at ASU’s Ira Fulton School of Engineering.
